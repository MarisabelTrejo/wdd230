Purpose Target Audience Design

Target Audience

Residents: Local residents of Garden City who are interested in connecting with businesses or events for their business.

Business Owners: Local business owners in Garden City who are looking to network with other companies, therefore, joining the chamber of commerce. Given benefits and resources from the organization.

Personas

John - Farmer

John is a farmer here in South West Kansas. He is looking for young people who are seeking internships, so that he could have help as well as teach and give real life experiences to those studying in agriculture.

Austin - Business Owner

Austin is a business owner and wants to get the word out about his shop. He wants to sell his product in the fall festival so the community can get familiar with it. His business can then start to flourish. He needs information on how to be a part of festival or speak to the city event cordinator.
